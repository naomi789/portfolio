About me



Introduction: Naomi Johnson graduated with a bachelor's in Computer Science and worked in software engineering before pivoting into UX Research. She has worked as a UXR in various industries (ecommerce, cyber security, education technology) and studied Human Centered Design Engineering at the University of Washington. She spends her free time volunteering as a UXR mentor on ADPList, piecing quilts, and taking portraits.

UXR Philosophy:While many people think about data, findings, and insight when planning UX Research, I believe the most important step is creating impact after the research is "finished". In order to maximize impact, I believe in democratizing research and I priorize offering stakeholders opportunities to collaborate and be engaged throughout every stage of research, when possible.
